# Research Index

Purpose: Route agents to non-authoritative research contracts and latent
proposals that may later promote into specs, runbooks, references, decisions, or
evidence.

Question this index answers: "what is being evaluated but is not yet
authoritative?"

## Use This Index When

- You need exploratory context before changing an authoritative concept.
- You are comparing options that have not yet been accepted.
- You need to promote a researched idea into a durable lane.

## Do Not Use This Index When

- You need the current authoritative contract.
- You need executable steps.
- You need proof that a current claim is still aligned.

## What Belongs In `docs/research/`

- Research contracts.
- Bounded option comparisons.
- Non-authoritative proposals that may later promote.

## Research Contract

Research concepts must include OKF frontmatter and the required sections listed
in `docs/policy.md`.

Use `promotes_to` only when the research has an explicit target lane.
